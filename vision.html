Vision and Purpose

Our core purpose is to provide high quality services for the development of the communities in which we operate. Our vision is to be the leading, diversified Infrastructure/ Energy group focused on Nigeria and selected African countries.

Mission

By leveraging a unique combination of our heritage, our status as a Nigerian based black owned company, our valued team of talented employees and our diverse range of capabilities, Unicorn Biogas aims to diversify and grow in a sustainable way to become a leading energy, environmental, catering and infrastructure services group in Nigeria and selected African countries, and thereby create stakeholder value.

SUSTAINABILITY

Recognizing the symbiotic relationship between business sustainability and the sustainable functioning of its social surroundings, Unicorn Biogas has a solid track record of corporate citizenship, which has been embedded in the business over the past few years.

Our danger purpose in society remains the delivery of services that enables economic and social development, in a sustainable way. This dual focus helps to ensure a sustainable future for our business, while creating shared value for our clients, our employees and positively impacting the communities in which we operate.

Sustainable development within Unicorn Biogas encompasses the commitment of leadership, business units and all employees. Sustainable business practice is embodied in our attitude towards Health, Safety & Environment (HSE); Transformation; and Ethical Governance. Each of these areas are embedded within our core business, are interconnected and contribute to creating shareholder value.

Unicorn Biogas’s Integrated Management System ensures our divisions adopt the most stringent standards and compliance to BS EN ISO 900:200 in addition to compliance to our client’s management plans and local and national laws.

Values

Unicorn Biogas’s values guide behavior and conduct and ensure alignment of the behavior of all Unicorn Biogas employees.

Care

We demonstrate care for:

  1. Our people by prioritizing their health, wellbeing and safety above all else;
  2. Our community and the environment by engaging local people and minimizing the potentially negative impacts of our activities, promoting sustainable solutions and positively contributing to broader society.

Trust

We create trust by:

  1. Living our values and conducting our business honestly and ethically; and
  2. Empowering employees and ensuring openness between employees, teams, clients and partners.

Delivery

We demonstrate delivery by:

  1. Meeting commitments to clients in terms of time, budget, quality and innovative solutions; and
  2. Taking responsibility for our actions and ensuring we deliver on what we promised.

Agility

We demonstrate agility by:

  1. Being flexible, innovative and responding to change;
  2. Continuously reassessing and learning
  3. Simplifying systems and focusing on people and results, over administration.

Teamwork

We demonstrate teamwork by:

  1. Nurturing strong relationships with employees, clients, partners and communities
  2. Building empowered work teams based on respect, dignity, diversity, and learning and development.
